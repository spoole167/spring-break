Not ready to migrate?

Spring Boot 3.5 stops receiving fixes when open-source support ends. You have three sane options. Pick one deliberately.

  1. Pin 3.5 and track CVEs. Free, but the clock is running and every new CVE is your team's problem to triage.
  2. Migrate incrementally with OpenRewrite. The Boot 4.0 recipe automates the renames in this book; the behavioural changes still need eyes.
  3. HeroDevs NES for Spring. Security patches for 3.5 beyond EOL, buying your team time to migrate on your schedule, not the calendar's.
